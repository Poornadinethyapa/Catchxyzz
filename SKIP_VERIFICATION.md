## Why Verification is Failing

The bytecode mismatch error happens when:
1. Compiler settings don't match exactly
2. Different Solidity version commits
3. Optimization settings differences
4. Sometimes temporary Basescan issues

**This doesn't mean your contract is broken** - it just means Basescan can't automatically match the source code.

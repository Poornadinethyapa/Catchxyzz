## Option 1: Base Build Dashboard (Recommended)

1. **Go to Base Build:**
   - Visit: https://www.base.dev
   - Or: https://base.dev (without www)

2. **Sign in:**
   - Click "Sign In" or "Connect Wallet"
   - Connect with your wallet (MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, etc.)
   - Or sign in with your Farcaster account if that option exists

3. **Navigate to Mini Apps:**
   - Look for "Mini Apps" or "Build" in the menu
   - Or go directly to: https://base.dev/mini-apps (if available)

4. **Create/Manage Your Mini App:**
   - Click "Create Mini App" or "Manage Mini Apps"
   - Enter your domain: `catchxyz.vercel.app`
   - You'll see the manifest management page

---

## Option 2: Farcaster Developer Tools

1. **Go to Farcaster Developer Tools:**
   - Visit: https://farcaster.xyz/~/developers/mini-apps/manifest
   - Or search for "Farcaster Developer Tools" on Google

2. **Sign in with Farcaster:**
   - Click "Sign In" or "Connect"
   - Use your Farcaster account

3. **Manage Your Manifest:**
   - Enter your domain: `catchxyz.vercel.app`
   - Follow the instructions to create/manage your manifest

---
